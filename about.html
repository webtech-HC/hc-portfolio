About Me

Hello! I’m Heather Cooper, a passionate Web Designer and Developer with a love for crafting beautiful, user-friendly, and accessible websites. My journey began with a deep curiosity for design and technology, and over the years, I’ve built skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with a focus on responsive layouts and intuitive user experiences.
